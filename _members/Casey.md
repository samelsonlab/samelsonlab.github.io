At JMU, I worked in the lab of Dr. George Vidal where I studied the cortical connectivity of the mouse brain in the context of neurodevelopmental disorders. After graduating, I moved to Berkeley, California and started my career in industry as a Research Associate at Octant Bio, a small molecule drug discovery startup. I also was also responsible for developing novel pharmacology and toxicology assays to support the program as we transitioned out of high throughput screening and into clinically enabling studies with our drug candidate OCT-980.
